About KSG ERP

Our Story

Founded in 2010, KSG ERP began as a small team of passionate developers and business experts in Kishangarh, Rajasthan. We recognized that many local businesses were struggling with inefficient processes and disconnected systems.

What started as custom solutions for local manufacturers has grown into a comprehensive ERP platform serving businesses across India. Today, we're proud to support over 500+ businesses in streamlining their operations.
